Their Story

Sameer and Aleena met during college fest preparations. Sameer was organizing the music event while Aleena was part of the photography team. Their first conversation was about balancing creativity with responsibilities.

As they worked together, they discovered shared interests in indie music, street food adventures, and late-night conversations about life. Their friendship gradually blossomed into a sweet romance filled with small gestures, inside jokes, and mutual support.

Now they navigate the beautiful complexities of teenage life together - balancing studies, dreams, and this new relationship that brings color to their everyday moments.
